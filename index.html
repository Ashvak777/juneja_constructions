Featured

Legal basements & basement finishing

A legal basement adds value and rental income — but only when egress, fire separation, and municipal rules are done right. That is where we focus.

Benefits

  • Compliance with Ontario building and fire requirements for secondary units where applicable
  • Stronger resale and rental appeal in Brampton and the GTA
  • Thoughtful layouts for landlords, families, and basement investors
  • Coordinated trades so drywall, electrical rough-ins, and finishes align

Why permits matter

Permits protect you from fines, insurance gaps, and costly rework. We plan for inspections up front so your suite is not just beautiful — it is defensible when it counts.
